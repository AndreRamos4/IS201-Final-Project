International Cinema

International Cinema is a subject that I am very passionate about. Movies are a gateway to new worlds and adventures that can offer some of the most memorable moments of our lives. Too many people miss out on these moments because of their aversion to movies not in English.

It has never been a better time to start watching with restoration projects of several classics. Ran is a recent example with it being restored in 4k and looking truly stunning.
